Our story

Serving koi and marine enthusiasts for decades.

Blue Ribbon Koi & Marine started as a small aquarium counter and grew into one of the region's most trusted names in freshwater koi and reef husbandry — over 4,000 gallons of saltwater display tanks, SPS, LPS, and softy coral colonies, and a koi yard stocked with imported Japanese tosai. Every fish that leaves our store has been quarantined, fed, and watched by people who genuinely love this work.
